Background

Betkong is an international esports bet exchange that's currently under development. I was approached to put together early UI and UX concepts for Betkong in Sketch.

Good design helps a brand exude trustworthiness and put people at ease, which is especially important when a) breaking into a market and b) money is involved. My approach was to marry great design with familiar gaming elements and an information structure that users would be familiar with.

  • Project Name Betkong
  • Skills Used User Interface Design, User Experience Design
  • Accomplished With Sketch 3, Photoshop
